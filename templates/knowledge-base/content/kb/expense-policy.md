# Expense policy

The short version: **spend the company's money like it's your own, keep the
receipt, file it the same week.**

## What's covered

- Travel to customer sites and conferences (economy / second class).
- Meals while traveling, within reason.
- Software and hardware your manager approved beforehand.
- Books and courses related to your work — no pre-approval needed under €50.

## What's not

- Alcohol, fines, upgrades, or anything you'd hesitate to explain out loud.

## How to file

1. Photograph the receipt the day you get it.
2. File it in the expenses app with the right category.
3. An admin approves it (you'll get an email either way).
4. Approved expenses are reimbursed with the next payroll run.
