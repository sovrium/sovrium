# Security policy

Security is everyone's job, and the rules are deliberately few and firm.

## The rules

1. **Passwords live in the vault.** Never in files, chats, or browsers'
   built-in storage on shared machines.
2. **Two-factor everywhere it's offered.** No exceptions for "quick" accounts.
3. **Lock your screen** when you step away — even at home.
4. **Report incidents immediately.** A false alarm costs minutes; a hidden
   incident costs weeks. There is no blame for reporting.
5. **Company data stays on company infrastructure.** No forwarding work
   documents to personal accounts or third-party tools that haven't been
   approved.

## Phishing

When an email feels off — urgency, odd sender, unexpected attachment — don't
click. Forward it to IT and delete it. You will never be penalized for
double-checking.
