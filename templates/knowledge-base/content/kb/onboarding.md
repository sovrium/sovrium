# Your first week

Welcome aboard! Here's the path through week one.

## Day 1 — accounts and access

1. Sign in to the intranet and this handbook with your company email.
2. Ask IT for your hardware (see [Setting up your laptop](/kb/it-setup)).
3. Meet your onboarding buddy — they're your first stop for any question.

## Day 2–3 — context

- Read the team's current quarter goals.
- Shadow one customer call or support session.
- Set up your development or work environment with your buddy.

## Day 4–5 — first contribution

Pick a small, real task with your manager. Shipping something in week one —
however small — beats a week of reading.

## Good to know

- Time off is requested through the HR app; approvals usually land same-day.
- Expenses go through the expenses app — photograph receipts as you go.
